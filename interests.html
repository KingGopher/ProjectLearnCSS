About My Interests

I don't really have all that many interests and what I do have I do a lot of. I spend as much time as I can on drawing, but gaming with friends is a
great way to releive stress so I end up doing that more than I would like. However, I do enough of each to get better little by little (except for
YouTube, I just watch that).

I've been drawing for about a year now and I've made a signifigant amount of progress, and I wish I had started at a younger age so I could be even beter
than I am now. I started drawing because I used to be into 3d modeling but I couldn't make humans, so I was suggested to start drawing people to help
get better at it, I ended up having more fun with drawing. I've been into Computer science since 7th grade when I learned how to make Windows batch
files. I would make text-based adventure games for me and my brother. I then learned about modding minecraft, and decided to try it myself... I was
a little overwhelmed having never touched anything like Java before. So I went back and learned C# and that went way smoother. I moved on to Unity
and even went to HTML for a breif bit. In those 8 years though, I didn't learn half as much as I have here at Neumont.
